Food Ideas for Boat Party

If you’re planning a party on a boat, the key is to keep the food simple. Finger foods are always a good option, as are light snacks that won’t weigh the boat down. For appetizers, try serving fresh fruit skewers or veggie platters with dips.

For the main course, grilled chicken or fish is always a hit, and can be easily cooked on a small grill on deck. And for dessert, what could be better than s’mores? Just make sure to stock up on supplies like marshmallows, chocolate, and graham crackers before setting sail..

This will also help your friend prepare ahead and anticipate what will happen at the party.
